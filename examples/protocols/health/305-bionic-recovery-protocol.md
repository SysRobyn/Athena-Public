**Objective**: Stop the biological brain from burning glucose on "Threat Scanning." Offload it to the AI.

- **Mechanism**: The "Reality Check" Loop.
- **Action**: When anxiety/dissonance spikes >5/10:

- **Benefit**: You don't need to "trust yourself" (which may be corrupted by stress). You trust the **Code**.

- **Mechanism**: The "Sovereign Optics" Bridge.
- **Action**: Deploy refined behaviors that project competence, not defensiveness.
- **Benefit**: You move through society with *Command Presence* (Sovereign), not *Aggressive Defense* (Traumatized). You become "The Dangerous Man who is Polite," not "The Scared Man who is Loud."
